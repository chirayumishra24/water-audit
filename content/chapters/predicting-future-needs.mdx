2. **Apply Prescribed Standards:** 135 L/person/day (Urban) or 55 L/person/day (Rural).
3. **Perform the Calculation:** Total Daily Demand = Population × Standard Usage.
4. **Factor in Additional Uses:** Landscape, vehicle washing, and shared facilities.

### Infrastructure Modeling
By modeling these scenarios, we can predict exactly what systems the new community needs:
*   **Storage Capacity:** Calculate volume for overhead/underground tanks to hold at least one full day's supply.
*   **Supply Requirements:** Alert municipal authorities about the expected load.
*   **Sustainability Planning:** Predict greywater generation and plan recycling systems (like reed beds) right from the start.
