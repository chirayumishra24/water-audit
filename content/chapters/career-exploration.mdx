### Essential Skills You've Developed
The activities in this project have helped you gain core competencies required for these roles:
*   **Data Analysis:** Collecting, organizing, and interpreting primary and secondary data.
*   **Scenario Modeling:** Using trends to predict future water demands for growing populations.
*   **Technical Knowledge:** Understanding plumbing, flow rates, and natural filtration like reed beds.
*   **Communication & Advocacy:** Writing reports and letters to influence policy and infrastructure.

### What Else Can I Do?
After finishing your project, consider these next steps:
1.  **Programming:** Write a program to help users estimate water consumption patterns.
2.  **Advocacy:** Write a formal letter to government officials with your audit findings.
3.  **Cross-Audit:** Apply your auditing skills to groceries, food waste, or electricity.
4.  **Advanced Tech:** Study solar water tanks and thermal collectors for residential use.
