### The Capstone Mission

Your journey through the Water Audit course has equipped you with critical tools—measurement skills, flow rate calculations, conservation strategies, and policy awareness. Now, it is time to bring these elements together. 

In this capstone project, you will step into the shoes of an expert water sustainability consultant. You will compile your findings and produce a professional-grade audit package.

### Your Objectives

<section>
  *   **Primary Data Capture:** Use the real flow rates, consumption volumes, and fixture counts from your home or school.
  *   **Secondary Context:** Factor in local average rainfall (in mm) and municipal supply metrics.
  *   **Analytical Synthesis:** Estimate the total volume of daily wastage and compare your per capita consumption against regional standards.
  *   **Predictive Modeling:** Calculate how changes in local population and rainfall patterns will impact the regional supply over the next decade.
  *   **Advocacy Output:** Generate a formal letter to officials outlining concrete steps for repair, greywater use, and rainwater harvesting.
</section>
