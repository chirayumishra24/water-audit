### Advocacy: The Bridge to Action
In the final stage of your water audit, **advocacy** serves as the bridge between your data collection and real-world change. By communicating your findings to government officials—such as your Ward Officer, Gram Panchayat, or Municipal Commissioner—you transform your project into a tool for community improvement.

### Proposed Suggestions for Officials
Offer practical solutions based on your audit findings:
*   **Fixing Infrastructure:** Immediate repair of leaking public pipes and taps.
*   **Greywater Systems:** Promoting the use of "greywater" for community gardens.
*   **Recharge Initiatives:** Recharging borewells near public hand pumps.
*   **Awareness & Tech:** Installing flow restrictors or aerators in public buildings.
