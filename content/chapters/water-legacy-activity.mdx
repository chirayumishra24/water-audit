# Activity: Water Legacy Architect

The final stage of a water audit is moving from the "what" to the "how." How do we ensure these findings lead to permanent change? How do we build a legacy of efficiency?

In this activity, you will use the **Water Legacy Architect** simulation to manage a facility's transformation over several decades. You will need to decide which projects to prioritize and how to communicate their value to stakeholders.

### Mission Objectives

1.  **Analyze the Roadmap**: Look at the timeline of projects available for your site.
2.  **Strategic Investment**: Decide which projects offer the best balance of water savings and community impact.
3.  **Monitor Progress**: Observe how your decisions affect the facility's water score over 10, 20, and 30 years.
4.  **Finalize the Vision**: Prepare to translate these findings into your final audit report.
