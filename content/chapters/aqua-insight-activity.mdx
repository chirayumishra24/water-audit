# Activity: Aqua Insight Wizard

Now that you have mastered manual data collection and interpretation, it's time to leverage advanced digital tools. The **Aqua Insight Wizard** is designed to process multiple data streams—from billing cycles to real-time sensor logs—and reveal the "story" behind the water.

In this activity, you will use the simulation below to explore a mock facility's data. Pay close attention to the peak demand hours and how they correlate with facility operations.

<ExternalSimulation 
  url="https://aqua-insight-wizard.lovable.app" 
  title="Aqua Insight Wizard"
  description="Advanced data analysis and insight generation simulation."
/>

### Instructions

1.  **Launch the Wizard**: Click into the simulation above to begin.
2.  **Select a Facility**: Choose the 'Industrial Complex' or 'Academic Campus' scenario.
3.  **Audit the Data**: Navigate through the 'Consumption' and 'Efficiency' tabs.
4.  **Identify Anomalies**: Look for the red indicators—these suggest areas where the actual consumption exceeds the predicted baseline.
5.  **Reflect**: How does this digital overview compare to the physical site walkthrough you performed in the previous chapter?
