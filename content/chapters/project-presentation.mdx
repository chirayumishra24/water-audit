## Project B: Project Presentation

### 🎤 The "Pitch": The Virtual Demo Day

**Students will record a 3-minute "Video Pitch" (Loom or Flip) or present live via Zoom/Teams.**

#### The Pitch Script Structure:

1.  **The Digital Hook:** "Did you know our neighborhood wastes enough water to fill three Olympic pools every month? We found the data to prove it."
2.  **The Tech Demo:** Screen-share the dashboard or map. "Our tool shows that 60% of our water goes to luxury uses while 10% of households face zero-pressure days."
3.  **The Impact:** "By using our digital dashboard, we can reduce 'Apparent Losses' (meter errors) by 15% through better reporting."
4.  **The Call to Action:** "We want the Housing Society to adopt this dashboard as our official monitoring tool."

---

### 🛠️ The "Pitch Deck" Template

To make it professional, students must follow this 5-slide structure:

*   **Slide 1: Problem Statement** (The NITI Aayog context + Local Audit findings).
*   **Slide 2: The Data Gap** (Show the difference between what people think they use and the actual flow rates measured).
*   **Slide 3: The Solution** (The Demo of their Dashboard, Map, or App).
*   **Slide 4: The Math** (A table showing projected savings in Cubic Meters (m³)).
*   **Slide 5: The Team & Vision** (How they will scale this to the whole school or district).
