## Project A: 💻 The Digital Project: "Water-Wise Tech Challenge"

### 1. The "Make" (Choose One Digital Path)

#### Path A: The Community Water Dashboard (Data Science)

*   **The Tool:** Google Sheets or Canva.
*   **The Task:** Take the raw data from the 50-family survey and build an interactive "State of the Water" Dashboard.
*   **What to include:**
    *   Dynamic charts showing "The Thirstiest Neighborhoods."
    *   A "Leakage Calculator" where users can input their flow rate and see their yearly waste.
    *   Visual benchmarks comparing local use to the Jal Jeevan Mission standards.

#### Path B: The "Source-to-Tap" Virtual Map (Geospatial Mapping)

*   **The Tool:** Google My Maps or StoryMaps.
*   **The Task:** Create a digital map of the local water ecosystem.
*   **What to include:**
    *   Pins for the local dam, the treatment plant, and the main distribution pipes.
    *   Data overlays showing groundwater depletion levels in different parts of the city using Secondary Data from the Central Ground Water Board.

#### Path C: The Digital "Nudge" Campaign (Social Innovation)

*   **The Tool:** Adobe Express or Canva
*   **The Task:** Design a digital platform to change community behavior.
*   **What to include:**
    *   Short "How-To" videos on measuring flow rates.
    *   A "Water Hero" leaderboard for families who reduce their per capita consumption.
